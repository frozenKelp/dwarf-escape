# dwarf escape

A 2D game made in **Godot 4** for the *Very Serious Juniper Dev* jam.\
**Theme:** "Spin to Win."

---

## the Emotional Backstory

A dwarven blacksmith ran up a mountain of gambling debt — and the dwarf
gambling king came to collect. Now he's locked deep inside a mountain, in an
automated refining facility, with no way out.

The deal is simple: work off the debt. Dig ore from the facility's mine, refine
it on the old dwarven tech, and sell the metal back to the people who put you
here. Pay it all down and they let you go.

The only real goal: **ESCAPE.**

The cruel joke is the slot machine humming away in the corner of the refinery —
the very thing that ruined him, still sitting there, still tempting. Hit the
jackpot and you could buy your freedom outright. Or lose everything again.

## The facility

Four rooms, one mountain, one way to spend your time:

- **The Refinery** — the heart of it all. Home to the great centrifuge smelter
  and, in the corner, that slot machine.
- **The Mine** — raw metal in the rock, worked by a dwarven wheel excavator.
- **The Delivery Room** — sell what you refine, buy gear, hand over debt
  payments. The boss's man works this room.
- **The Bedroom** — sleep to pass the day. The old fan barely turns; you have to
  coax it.

## The centrifuge smelter

The facility's prize: proprietary dwarven metallurgy no modern tech can match.
Metal melts in the centre, runs down the shaft into the cast, and centrifugal
force flings it outward at enormous speed — high-pressure casting that crushes
out impurities as it goes. Load the feeder to mix alloys.

**You spin it by hand.** Grab the cast with the mouse and whirl it in circles
(staying outside the inner ring) — the centrifuge chases the speed of your
hand. Hold it steady in the right band and the metal comes out clean. Push it
too hard and the cast turns unstable. Get it right, stop the spin, and sell the
result.

In the right hands it forges what the world has forgotten: **frost iron**,
**millennial steel**, **mithril** — and, per ancient inscriptions, even
**orichalcum**, whose exact ratio was lost to time.

## Ways out

| Route | Notes |
|---|---|
| **Pay off the debt** | The honest grind. The one the game tells you about. |
| **Hit the jackpot** | The slot machine could buy your freedom. *(hidden)* |
| **Dig out** | Mine your own tunnel through the mountain. *(hidden)* |
| **Rediscover orichalcum** | Relearn the lost ratio. The hardest path. *(hidden)* |
| **Bribe the delivery guy** | He plays along — then sells you out to the boss. *(a trap)* |

## Theme

*Spin to win*, all the way down: the centrifuge spins, the slot machine spins,
the excavator wheel spins, even the bedroom fan spins. Spinning is the whole
fantasy.
